Cactus

Cacti can symbolize the loneliness of the desert, but they are more well known for their resilience. Cacti can endure harsh conditions and overcome any challenges, proving their strength.

Roses come in all different colors and each variation has something to do with the concept of love. Roses are quite common, but still universally loved!

Sunflowers

Sunflowers are known for their positivity and always looking towards the sun. They also represent good luck and happiness.
